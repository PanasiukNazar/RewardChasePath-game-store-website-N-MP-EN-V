Privacy Policy

Introduction

We respect and prioritize the privacy and protection of personal data of our customers and visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information in relation to purchasing PC and console games through our online store. By using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

When you use our services to purchase PC and console games, we may collect personal data such as your full name, contact details, and any other information you choose to provide. This may happen when filling out forms, registering for an account, or contacting us through email or support systems.

2. Usage Data

We may collect data related to your interaction with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and timestamps. This helps us understand user behavior and enhance the performance of our store.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies and related technologies to improve user experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze site usage. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings, but disabling them may impact some functionalities.

How We Use Your Information

1. Providing Services

We use your personal data to deliver our services, including order processing, customer support, game delivery, and user account management.

2. Communication

Your contact details may be used to send service updates, promotional offers, and relevant information about new PC and console game releases. You may opt out of marketing messages at any time.

3. Analytics and Improvement

Usage data and cookies are used to analyze trends, administer the website, and optimize our content and user interface for a better shopping experience.

4. Legal Compliance

We may use your data to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our terms of service. Data may be disclosed to legal authorities if required by law.

Data Sharing

1. Third-Party Services

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist in website operations or order fulfillment. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may not use it for other purposes.

2. Business Transfers

In the event of a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you and ensure your rights are maintained.

3. Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to secure your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We recommend using strong passwords and being cautious when sharing data online.

Your Rights

1. Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information. If any data is inaccurate or incomplete, please contact us to make updates.

2. Deletion

You may request the deletion of your personal data unless we are legally required to retain it.

3. Restriction of Processing

You may request the restriction of your data processing in specific cases, such as disputes over data accuracy.

4. Data Portability

You may request a copy of your personal information in a portable format to transfer it to another service provider.

5. Objection

You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for marketing or other legitimate interests. We will respect your choice and adjust processing accordingly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Please review this page periodically for the latest updates.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your personal data, please contact us. We are here to assist you.
